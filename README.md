##  <span style="color:#20c997;">Project Overview</span>

Ferns and Petals (FNP) is a popular gifting platform in India, offering gifts for various occasions such as **Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries**. This project involves a comprehensive analysis of FNP‚Äôs dataset to extract insights on:

- Customer behavior
- Sales trends across months and occasions
- Product performance and popularity
- Delivery patterns and efficiencies

The ultimate goal is to help FNP make data-driven decisions to enhance customer satisfaction and increase revenue.

---

##  <span style="color:#20c997;">Key Business Questions Answered</span>

1. Total Revenue - Identify the overall revenue generated by all orders.
2. verage Order and Delivery Time - Evaluate the average time taken from order placement to delivery.
3. Monthly Sales Performance (2023)- Analyze how monthly sales fluctuate throughout the year 2023.
4. Top Products by Revenue - Identify the products that bring in the highest revenue.
5. Customer Spending Analysis - Understand how much customers spend on average per order.
6. Sales Performance by Top 5 Products - Track how the top 5 revenue-generating products perform over time.
7. Top 10 Cities by Number of Orders - Find the cities with the highest number of orders placed.
8. Order Quantity vs Delivery Time - Explore whether larger order quantities lead to longer delivery times.
9. Revenue Comparison Between Occasions - Compare the total revenue generated during each major occasion.
10. Product Popularity by Occasion - Reveal which products are most favored during specific occasions.

- Extracting hour of the day as hour ( order Hour) column in order table from order time  for peak hour analysis
- Extracting hour of the day as hour ( Delivery Hour) column in order table from delivery time  for delivery hour analysis
- Creating a calculated column to calculate the difference of days between order placed date and delivery date as Delivery days.
- Using merge query to bring the product price into order table so we can calculate revenue
